Who Am I?

I like the challenge and continuous learning that defines the tech world. In a field where the only constant is change, I see every new complexity as an opportunity for growth and optimization.

Over the past five years, I've worked building simple, efficient, and data-driven solutions. I am an analytical —the person who still reads the 'manual' (documentation!)—because being well-informed is the foundation of engineering.

I view tools like AI as incredibly helpful aids for coding, accelerating development and reducing boilerplate. Crucially, I treat AI as a powerful assistant, not a crutch; my own fundamental understanding and problem-solving skills remain the core of my work.

Ultimately, I believe the power of software is limited only by imagination and a willingness to learn. I'm excited to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible, whether it's optimizing a complex API or tackling an entirely new framework.
